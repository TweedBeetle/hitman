The thumbnail to a youtube video will be comprised of some Text and and a single image.
The task here is to find a suitable image for the thumbnail, given the video's title.

Examples for suitable images include:

  • An image of a face showing the prevailing emotion of the video title
  • An Image showing an example of something mentioned in the title
  • An image showing a possible reaction of someone watching the video

When selecting an image, the following factors must be taken into account:

  • The image must be appropriate for youtube
  • The image should be eye-catching
  • Ideally, the image should be in portrait (taller than it is wide)
  • Ideally, the image background should be a single solid color
  • The image must not contain watermarks

Only direct links to images ending in '.png' or '.jpg' will be deemed valid.
When pasted in the url bar, the link must yield a page showing only the image.
